Welcome

Welcome to our exploration of gender dynamics in the context of war. This website delves into how gender roles and identities are shaped, challenged, and redefined through conflicts. Our focus is on three critical areas:

Topics

Women and Gender in Terrorism and Counter-Terrorism

This section examines the roles of women in terrorist activities and counter-terrorism efforts. Topics include the strategic use of women as suicide bombers, gender-specific recruitment tactics by terrorist organizations, and the importance of incorporating gender perspectives in counter-terrorism strategies.

Women in War

Here, we explore the diverse roles women have played in wars throughout history and in contemporary conflicts. We highlight examples of women in combat and leadership positions, as well as their vital contributions in support roles such as nursing and logistics.

Gendered Impacts of War

This section investigates how wars impact men and women differently. We discuss the use of sexual violence as a weapon of war, the societal perceptions of gender roles in conflict, and the long-term consequences of war on women's lives, including displacement and psychological trauma.
